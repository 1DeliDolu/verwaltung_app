## 99. Collapsible Department Management Forms Verification

1. `php -l resources/views/departments/show.php` calistirildi.
2. `php -l resources/views/layouts/app.php` calistirildi.
3. Departman yonetim panellerinin `collapse` yapisina tasindigi dogrulandi.
4. Toggle butonlari icin ortak `department-form-toggle` ve `department-form-toggle-icon` stillerinin eklendigi dogrulandi.
5. URL hash ile hedeflenen panelin acilmasi icin layout icine Bootstrap `Collapse` tabanli acilis script'i eklendigi dogrulandi.
